Welcome to Sweetness & Celebrations

After years of crafting cakes for birthday parties,anniversarys and other events,I learned a whole lot about the process of cake creation,experimenting with recipes, and—most importantly—getting to taste delicious cake. While volunteering to make cakes for anyone and everyone, I began to blend my love for painting and sculpture with love for baking and decorating.I believes that cake is the tastiest art form.You may even find yourself smiling from all the sweetness.
